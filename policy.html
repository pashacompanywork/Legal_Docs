Information We Collect

User-entered content. All content you enter into the Application (such as names, dates, and notes about your matches) is stored only on your device. This data is never transmitted to the Service Provider or any third party.

Technical data collected by third-party SDKs. The Application uses third-party services for subscription management and paywall functionality. These services may automatically collect limited technical data, including:

  • Anonymous user identifiers (not linked to your name or email)
  • Device identifiers and device model

  • IP address (used for approximate country-level geolocation)

  • Paywall interaction events (e.g., views, taps)
  • App version and SDK version

The Application does not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.
