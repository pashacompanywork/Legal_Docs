FAQ

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. If you delete the app, your data is deleted with it.

How do I unlock pattern insights?

Insights unlock as you log more matches and complete post-date debriefs. Each insight card shows how many more entries you need before it unlocks.

Can I export or back up my data?

There is no export feature at this time. Your data lives on your device, so we recommend keeping regular device backups via iCloud or your computer.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone and select Dately to manage or cancel. Apple handles all subscription billing.

I'm not receiving date reminders.

Make sure notifications are enabled for Dately in your device Settings > Notifications. Also check that reminders are turned on inside the app's settings.

Can I restore my purchase on a new device?

Yes. Open the app, and your subscription will be restored automatically if you're signed in with the same Apple ID. If not, the paywall screen has a "Restore Purchases" option.
